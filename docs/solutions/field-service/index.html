The Challenge

Critical service knowledge remains locked in fragmented file structures, legacy SharePoint folders, and static PDFs. The result is measurable and costly:

  • Long resolution times — Engineers cannot quickly find the right diagnostic path for complex instrument failures
  • Inconsistent quality across geographies — Knowledge availability varies by region, team, and experience level
  • Costly escalations — Senior experts spend disproportionate time on issues resolvable at lower tiers
  • Low AI confidence — Corporate AI tools lack domain expertise and deliver generic answers
  • Pressure to do more with less — Faster, smarter service without expanding headcount

How Empower AI Solves It

Rapid Ontology Construction

Automatically builds a detailed, domain-specific knowledge graph from existing technical manuals, service logs, CRM notes, and support documents. The system learns your organization's own expert language.

Knowledge Fabric with Provenance

Every answer is grounded in a verifiable source. Engineers can see where information came from — document, paragraph, author, and version — building confidence and traceability.

AI-Driven Reasoning

Field engineers can ask complex, multi-dimensional questions and receive synthesized answers with cited evidence — resolving complex issues in seconds.

Agentic Automation

The platform integrates with existing systems (CRM, ticketing, service logs) to generate recommended actions, parts lists, and step-by-step guidance — seamlessly feeding field and back-office workflows.
