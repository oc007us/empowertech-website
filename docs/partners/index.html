Partner Tiers

Three tiers designed around deepening commitment, capability, and mutual investment.

Tier 1

Registered Partner

  • Sales enablement kit (deck, demos, one-pagers)
  • Access to reference architectures + best practices
  • Lead registration eligibility

Basic training + agreement

Tier 2

Certified Delivery Partner

Firms actively implementing for clients

  • Certification badge
  • Implementation accelerators + templates
  • Joint solution packaging (vertical kits)

Certified staff + first successful deployment

  • Co-selling + joint account planning

  • Joint marketing / case studies

Volume targets, quality SLAs, annual business plan
